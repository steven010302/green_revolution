Project Overview

Renewable Energies

In a world increasingly committed to sustainability and the fight against climate change, renewable energies have become key players in global energy development. Solar energy, wind energy, and green hydrogen represent clean, efficient, and low-impact alternatives to traditional fossil fuels.

Advantages & Global Comparison

This project explores how these energy sources are being implemented, their main technological, environmental, and economic advantages, and analyzes production and usage statistics in the leading countries in the energy transition: China, the United States, Germany, and Spain. A detailed analysis of Colombia is also included to compare its current situation and potential with these international benchmarks.

Project Goal

Our goal is to understand not only the technological progress but also the political and economic commitment driving the development of these energies. Through data, comparisons, and real-world implementation examples, we aim to visualize the path toward a cleaner, fairer, and more sustainable energy future.
